About Me

Experienced bioinformatician with a passion for my field and a keen interest in genomics and data science. My training started with genetics and progressed to bioinformatics, and I have since found great fulfillment in solving biological questions through computational methods.

I have strong data analysis and software development skills using R and Python, as well as workflow automation using Snakemake and Nextflow. I also have strong expertise in genomics and molecular biology, including cancer genomics.

I am currently working as operational bioinformatician at Zinto Ltd, an equine genetics company. In this role I have initiated and led several major projects including an overhaul of the current bioinformatics codebase and the introduction of a new LIMS system. I’m also responsible for day-to-day analysis of genetic data and for curation of the company’s large genetic database.
